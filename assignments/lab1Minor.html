The purpose of the IT minor program is to provide non-IT major students an opportunity to gain more computational skills and knowledge in IT.

Courses required for IT minor:

  1. ITEC 2140 - Programming Fundamentals
  2. ITEC 2201 - Introduction to Information Systems
  3. ITEC 3100 - Introduction to Networks
  4. ITEC 3200 - Introduction to Databases
  5. ITEC 3900 - Professional Practice and Ethics
